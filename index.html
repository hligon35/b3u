About B3U

Authentic veterans' voice, sharing real stories and experiences. Explore our featured episodes and get in touch for speaking engagements.

Bree Charles

Outspoken. Active. Dedicated.

B3U Podcast is hosted by Bree Charles, a retired veteran who believes in speaking truth, transparency and transitioning those mindsets that are in a state of defeat or confusion, on the path to their successful, purposeful lives. Our show features authentic conversations about life changing challenges, leadership, military service and the importance of our communities mindset shifts in order to become more powerful and UNSTOPPABLE!
